The sixth edition of this conference (Virtual Mode) provides an opportunity for the researchers, engineers, developers, and practitioners from academia and industry to discuss and address the experimental, theoretical work and methods in solving problems related to Data Science. Also, to share their experience and exchange their ideas in the field of Data Science and Computational Intelligence.
